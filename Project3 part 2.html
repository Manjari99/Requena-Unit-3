Nail Designs

There are different types of nails designs that you can do all by yourself. Some are quick and easy to do while others aren't. These are some youtubers that can help you.

If you are the type of person who wants pretty nail designs but think it might be difficult to do, well arcadia is the person to watch. She has many nail tutorials on simple nail designs. Arcadia

Sandi, better known as Cute polish, has lots of videos on what your looking for like nail art for beginners, character nail art, animal prints and many more. If you are interested in that click the link, which will take you to her youtube channel. Cute polish

If you are looking for nail designs for special occasions, holiday or just for a day at the beach, Janelle is the right person to go to. She has many videos on different and pretty nail art. Click the link to go to Janelle's channel. elleandish // Janelle
